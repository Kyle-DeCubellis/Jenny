On the Bike Since

Jenny's love of spinning started young — hopping on a bike alongside her mom as a teenager, just for fun. But the music, the energy, and the feeling of a room full of people pushing together stuck with her in a way nothing else did.

With a background in early childhood education, teaching was already in her DNA. In 2009, she brought those two passions together and started leading spin classes in Boston. She's been on the bike ever since — now with over 15 years of experience and a reputation for classes that are as fun as they are challenging.

Today, Jenny serves as Group Exercise Coordinator and Personal Trainer at The Westboro Club, where she helped build the Ultimate Group Exercise (UGX) program. Whether it's on the bike, in the weight room, or out on a run, she believes fitness should feel good — for the body and the mind.

Come ride with her. You'll be glad you did.
